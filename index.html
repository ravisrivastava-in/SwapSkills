About SwapSkills

A peer-to-peer skill exchange platform where knowledge is the only currency

Why SwapSkills?

Traditional learning can be expensive. SwapSkills eliminates cost barriers by connecting people who can teach each other.

  • Zero Cost — Exchange skills without any monetary transactions
  • Smart Matching — Our algorithm finds perfect skill exchange partners
  • Real-time Chat — Communicate instantly with your matched peers
  • Session Scheduling — Plan learning sessions at your convenience
  • Ratings & Reviews — Build trust through community feedback

How It Works

Getting started with SwapSkills is easy — just four simple steps

1

Create Profile

Sign up and list the skills you can teach and the skills you want to learn.

2

Get Matched

Our smart algorithm finds peers whose offered skills match what you want — and vice versa.

3

Connect & Chat

Accept matches and start chatting in real-time. Plan your sessions together.

4

Learn & Rate

Exchange knowledge in scheduled sessions. Rate each other to build trust.
